Advantis Dental Surgeries, LLC (ADS):

 Office Manager register Dentists who apply to join their network of dental surgeries
 Office Manager also uses the system to enroll new Patients who require dental services
 Patient can also request appointment by submitting an online form on the ADS website
 Office Manager can then book the appointment and the system will send a confirmation email  notifying the Patient and the appointment gets recorded accordingly
 Dentists should be able to sign-in to the system and view a listing of all their Appointments,
including details of the Patients who they have been scheduled to see
 Patients should be able to sign-in to the system and view their appointments, including the information of the dentist who they have been booked to see
 Patients should also be able to request to cancel or change their appointments.
 A dentist cannot be given more than 5 appointments in any given week
 The system should prevent a Patient from requesting a new appointment if they have an outstanding, unpaid bill for dental service they have received.
